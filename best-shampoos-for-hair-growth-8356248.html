The 7 Best Shampoos for Hair Growth of 2024

Whether you’re growing out your hair or want to revitalize strands after experiencing hair loss, taking care of the hair and the scalp can help you achieve your goals. Just like our complexions, a healthy scalp and strands all start with cleanliness, and shampoos specifically formulated for hair growth are where the magic happens. 

“While other factors may come into play like medications, treatments, diet, and health conditions, the way that you take care of your hair is key to its growth and health,” Dr. Dendy Engleman, a board-certified cosmetic dermatologist and Mohs surgeon at Shafer Clinic in NYC, tells PEOPLE. She explains that healthy hair is closely linked to a healthy scalp, hair care techniques, and hair products, which is why if you want longer, fuller strands, utilizing a shampoo for hair growth can help up the ante on your efforts. “Hair growth shampoos contain ingredients that are designed to support and stimulate hair growth, which can make a considerable difference in the rate of hair growth,” Dr. Engelman notes. 

Since hair growth is a popular goal, there are plenty — and we mean plenty — of hair growth shampoos available at our favorite beauty shopping destinations. So, how do you know which ones work best and will actually give you your desired results? The answer lies in trial and error, which is exactly what we did: Our PEOPLE Tested team researched and washed their hair with nearly two dozen formulas, carefully considering the ingredients, efficacy, and results to determine which hair growth shampoo formulas are worth buying. After much deliberation, we narrowed it down to the top seven shampoos for hair growth.

What to Look for in Shampoo for Hair Growth

Hair Growth Ingredients 

When shopping for an effective shampoo for hair growth, Dr. Engelman says to first consider the ingredients, especially those that can support a healthy scalp and hair growth. “Look for ingredients that are known to stimulate hair growth, including caffeine, peppermint, green tea, and biotin,” she explains. “These ingredients will allow the shampoo to do its job of helping your hair grow faster and more healthily.” In addition to these ingredients, ginseng is a popular hair growth shampoo ingredient that can reduce inflammation and extend the growing phase for longer, stronger strands. 

Our top pick for the best budget shampoo, the Marc Anthony Grow Long Super Fast Strength Shampoo, is enriched with ginseng and caffeine in its ingredient lineup. The DS Laboratories Antioxidant Hair Density CBD Shampoo also features caffeine while the best hydrating shampoo, the Alodia Nourish & Hydrate Creme' Shampoo ( Low Lather Sulfate Free), has peppermint for stimulation. 

Hair Strengthening Ingredients 

In addition to hair growth ingredients, Dr. Engelman also says to look for formulas with ingredients that will strengthen the hair follicles and cuticles. “Just as important as growing more hair is ensuring that the hair you have stays healthy and strong,” she explains. “Ingredients like keratin and argan oil help condition and strengthen the hair, making it more resistant to breakage and environmental stress.” 

Our top pick for the overall best shampoo for hair growth, the Vegamour GRO+ Advanced Replenishing Shampoo, features Karmatin™ in its ingredient lineup. This is a vegan keratin alternative that is designed to bond hair and support strand strength.

No Harmful Additives 

There’s a reason why dermatologists recommend shampoos free from harmful chemicals, such as sulfates, parabens, formaldehyde, phthalates, and even synthetic fragrances. “These ingredients can weaken and damage the hair and actually contribute to hair thinning and loss,” Dr. Engelman notes.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do hair growth shampoos actually work?

    Dr. Engelman says the results may vary. “In most cases, hair growth shampoos are a great way to promote and support healthy hair growth,” she explains. “They work by supplying the scalp with ingredients that stimulate the hair growth cycle and/or extend the anagen (active) phase of the hair growth cycle.” With this in mind, hair growth shampoos can also work effectively at preventing hair loss by strengthening the strands and cuticle. However, not everyone will see the same amount of growth as there are many nuances responsible for hair loss or stagnant growth.

    To that point, Dr. Michael I. Jacobs, a board-certified dermatologist and associate professor of dermatology at Weill Cornell Medical College and Medical Technology Director at Cortina, says it’s worth considering the cause of your hair loss. “Many external factors such as stress, medication, and hormones can cause hair loss,” he explains. If you experience hair loss persistently, Dr. Jacobs recommends seeking advice from a medical professional, as hair growth shampoos might not be enough to reverse the effects.  

  • How long will it take to see results from a hair growth shampoo?

    Just like with shampoos for thinning hair and other hair growth products, the amount of time it takes to see results from a hair growth shampoo can vary. On average, Dr. Engelmen says it can take approximately four to eight weeks of recommended use to experience results (a.k.a. new growth). With that said, as mentioned previously, it’s important to note that some hair loss might not be fixed by a shampoo alone, so if your hair loss is more persistent, it’s worth seeking advice from a medical professional to learn more about how you can address your specific hair loss situation. 

  • Which shampoo is best for hair growth and thickness?

    Our top pick for the best shampoo for hair growth is the Vegamour GRO+ Advanced Replenishing Shampoo. This shampoo features a keratin alternative and provides plenty of nourishment to restore hair health and even encourage wave patterns for those with wavy hair types. During the six-week testing period, we saw significant improvement in hair growth while using this formula; by the end of it, our tester's length grew by around 1.5 inches, and their baby hairs appeared thicker, too. 

    The Vegamour shampoo is on the more expensive side, so if you’re looking for a more budget-friendly option, we also recommend the Marc Anthony Grow Long Super Fast Strength Shampoo.
